I am a Programmer, Musician, Human

Everything is possible with sheer determination by your side!

There are lots of maxima and minima in my life. I was working with Citigroup - Global Consumer Technology domain with teams that launched Samsung Pay, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and Citi pay for all the APAC/EMEA markets. I very much loved the work, but God has his plans. When all my friends were planning to do an MBA, I wondered what's my next step in the quest to take risks & exploring options. In this perplexity, I went on to shop a couple of books as a habit and found this fantastic book Elon Musk - by Ashlee Vance. I can firmly say that it changed my perspectives on engineering and business management ( I highly recommend it to all the students out there trying to realize their purpose )

It helped in finding my purpose - Innovative engineering in the domain of Transportation. Tesla Inc. has inspired me not only to pursue higher studies in the field of A.I. (thanks to their state of the art - Autopilot) but also to plan to move to the U.S.

As Aristotle said, "Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet." I have been accepted to Rutgers University, New Brunswick, as a Masters's candidate in Computer Science concentrating Machine Learning - Trust me handling a full-time job and applying to universities is really an arduous task.
